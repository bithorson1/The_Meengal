About The Meengal

Bringing generations of coastal tradition and expertise to deliver the finest dry fish from India's pristine waters to your table.

Our Story

The Meengal was born from a deep-rooted passion for preserving the authentic flavors of coastal India. For generations, our families have been connected to the sea, understanding the delicate art of selecting, processing, and drying fish using time-honored traditional methods.

What started as a small family operation has grown into a trusted name in premium dry fish, but our commitment remains unchanged: to deliver only the highest quality products that honor our heritage and delight our customers.

"We don't just sell dry fish – we share a piece of our coastal heritage with every product."

Today, we work directly with local fishermen and coastal communities, ensuring sustainable practices while supporting their livelihoods. Every piece of dry fish we offer tells a story of tradition, quality, and care.

Our Process

From ocean to your table – how we ensure quality at every step

1

Sourcing

We work with trusted local fishermen who catch fish from pristine coastal waters using sustainable methods. Only the freshest catch is selected for processing.

2

Selection

Our experts carefully inspect each fish, selecting only premium quality specimens. We reject any fish that doesn't meet our strict standards.

3

Traditional Processing

Fish are cleaned and prepared using traditional methods, then sun-dried naturally. This preserves the authentic flavor and nutritional value.

4

Quality Check

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure proper drying, flavor, and hygiene standards before packaging.

5

Delivery

Products are carefully packaged to maintain freshness and delivered directly to your doorstep with care and efficiency.
