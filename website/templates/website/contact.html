Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order? +
You can place an order through our website by browsing our products, adding items to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. Alternatively, you can call us or send us a WhatsApp message with your requirements.
What are your delivery areas? +
We currently deliver across Chennai and surrounding areas. For orders outside Chennai, please contact us to check delivery availability and charges.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery within Chennai takes 1-2 business days. For areas outside Chennai, delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on the location.
Do you accept bulk orders? +
Yes! We welcome bulk orders and offer special pricing for large quantities. Please contact us directly with your requirements, and we'll provide you with a customized quote.
What is your return policy? +
We stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll work to resolve the issue promptly.
How should I store dry fish? +
Store dry fish in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. For longer shelf life, you can refrigerate or freeze them. Properly stored, our dry fish can last for several months while maintaining quality.
